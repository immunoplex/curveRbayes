Data-adaptive prior structure. MFI_max and MFI_range are computed from the preprocessed standards for the current curve_id set.
Parameter Population mean prior Population SD prior Rationale
log(A) Normal(log(0.01 × MFI_max), 1) Half-Normal(0, 1) Lower asymptote near background; broad on log scale.
log(B) Normal(log(1.5), 0.5) Half-Normal(0, 0.5) Hill slope near 1.5; constrained positive.
C (log10 scale) Normal(mid-range log10(x), 1) Half-Normal(0, 1) Inflection at geometric centre of concentration range.
log(D) Normal(log(MFI_max), 0.5) Half-Normal(0, 0.5) Upper asymptote near observed maximum.
sigma_obs Half-Normal(0, 0.1 × MFI_range) — Noise scaled relative to signal range.
